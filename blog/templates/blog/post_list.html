   Meet our Crew

Ashley Hutton

Ashley has always had a love of makeup and a natural ability in this field. She trained In basic makeup while studying hairdressing and beauty therapy at college after leaving school in 1997.

She's worked on various makeup counters before working in celebrity salon The Urban Retreat based in Harrods. There she trained with top makeup artist Daniel Sandler. When she moved back to Scotland she worked doing makeup for IS Magazine Fashion shoots and Boudoir Photographer Peach Fur. After five years of doing hair extensions and makeup on a mobile basis she decided to open up Glamour to have a permanent base but still offer a mobile service. The most recent course undertaken was the Professional HD MUA course in which she was one of the first in Scotland to pass.

Nowadays she is very busy with bridal parties most weekends due to her talent and the fact that she doesn't believe in charging brides more money just for being a bride. Ashley is definitely best known for her Glamorous makeup looks and her fabulous Smokey Eyes.
